About the exhibition

At UNLV Lee Business School, marketing is both strategy and creativity. This exhibition gives students a place to build portfolios, tell brand stories, test visual ideas, and present work with professional polish.

  • Strengthen your portfolio
  • Show your creative voice
  • Practice brand storytelling
  • Gain public exposure and feedback
